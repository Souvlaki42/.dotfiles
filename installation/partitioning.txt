// Windows 11 dual boot only!
fdisk -l
cfdisk /dev/<DISK NAME> (nvme0n1)
Create 1G EFI system partition out of free space
Create <RamSize>G (8G) linux swap out of free space
Create linux filysystem partition with the remaining free space
mkfs.fat -F32 /dev/<EFI partition> (nvme0n1p5)
mkfs.ext4 /dev/<Root partition> (nvme0n1p7)
mkswap /dev/<Swap partition> (nvme0n1p6)
swapon /dev/<Swap partition> (nvme0n1p6)
mount /dev/<Root partition> /mnt (nvme0n1p7)
mount /dev/<EFI partition> /mnt/boot (nvme0n1p5)
